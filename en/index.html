Consulting

Whether you want advice on the most performant models or simply on the possibilities offered by AI with your data, we support your teams at every stage of your data projects.

Feasibility Study

If you want to launch an AI project with your data, we offer to study your project by producing POCs (Proof of Concepts) and feasibility studies to guide you towards the best possibilities.

If your AI project has been active for several years, we compare your models with state-of-the-art techniques to help you evaluate the benefits of evolving your algorithms.
